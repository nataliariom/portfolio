Public projects I’ve contributed to

Native Business

Developed a fully responsive website for Native Business, a platform that supports Native American entrepreneurs and organizations. The project involved clear information architecture, performance optimization, and accessibility improvements. Focused on showcasing certifications, eligibility under the Buy Indian Act, and facilitating access to resources, forms, and downloadable brochures. The site was built with a clean layout, fast load times, and strategic call-to-action placements.

Anna Allier Jewerly

Developed a fully responsive and visually appealing jewelry e-commerce website using WordPress and WooCommerce. Focused on elegant UI design, product categorization, secure checkout, and user-friendly navigation. Implemented custom styling, mobile optimization, and integrations for payments, inventory, and shipping.

PayPocket Funnel

Designed and developed a responsive, mobile-first landing page for a fintech eBook using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The page presents the benefits of the PayPocket platform in a visually engaging, scroll-based storytelling format. Includes custom illustrations, animations, and call-to-actions to guide users toward downloading the eBook or exploring related services.

Zeal - Indor Cycling App

Mobile app developed with React Native, Expo Go, and EAS for managing class bookings at an indoor cycling studio. Available on Android and iOS, the app allows users to browse schedules, reserve sessions, and manage their profiles. The project included UI design implementation, API integration, and deployment through Google Play and App Store using EAS Build.
